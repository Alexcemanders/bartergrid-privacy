BarterGrid — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 20, 2026

BarterGrid ("the app", "we", "us") is a marketplace that lets local growers and neighbors barter fresh produce with one another. This policy explains what information the app collects, how it is used, and the choices you have. By using BarterGrid you agree to this policy.

Information we collect

  • Account identifier and PIN. When you sign in you choose a vendor ID and a 4-digit PIN. Your PIN is never stored in readable form — it is salted and hashed on our server.
  • Profile information. A display name and the produce you are offering and looking for.
  • Location. With your permission, the app reads your device's precise location so it can place you on the map and help arrange trade meet-ups. The location shown to other users is rounded to roughly a 100-meter area rather than your exact position.
  • Trade activity. Trade proposals you send or receive, including the items involved, a chosen meeting place and time, a brief description of yourself or your vehicle for identification at the meetup, and any notes you add.

How we use your information

We use the information solely to operate the app: to authenticate you, display produce listings and an approximate map of nearby users, let you propose and manage trades, and keep the service secure. We do not use your data for advertising and we do not sell your data.

Information shared with other users

Other signed-in users of BarterGrid can see your display name, the produce you offer and want, and your approximate location on the map. When you enter into a trade, the counterparty additionally sees the meeting place, time, and notes you provide for that trade. Your PIN, authentication token, and exact coordinates are never shown to other users.

Third-party services

The app relies on the following service providers, who process data only to provide their function:

  • Render (application hosting) and Neon (database hosting) — store the data described above.
  • Google Maps SDK — renders the map on your device; your use of the map is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • OpenStreetMap / Nominatim — when you browse the map, the app may query this service for nearby meeting places using the map area you are viewing.
  • Expo — delivers over-the-air app updates.

We do not send your identity or PIN to these map services.

Data security

All traffic between the app and our servers is encrypted with HTTPS/TLS. PINs are stored using PBKDF2 hashing, session tokens are stored in your device's secure keychain and expire automatically, and database connections are encrypted.

Data retention and deletion

We keep your account information for as long as your account exists.

Delete your BarterGrid account

You can delete your BarterGrid account and all associated data at any time. Here's how:

  1. Send an email to jeralexasiye@gmail.com from the email address you use, with the subject line "Delete my BarterGrid account".
  2. Include your BarterGrid username (shown on your Profile screen in the app) so we can locate your account.
  3. We will verify the request and permanently delete your data within 30 days. You'll receive a confirmation email once this is done.

What gets deleted: your username and profile (name, produce listings), your stored location, your PIN (hashed), your authentication tokens, and your trade history (proposals, meeting details, and notes) tied to your account.

What may be retained: trade records where you were the counterparty to another user's trade may be retained in de-identified form as part of that other user's trade history, since deleting your account does not delete data that belongs to someone else's records. No financial, health, or government-ID data is collected in the first place, so none is retained.

Retention period: account and profile data is deleted within 30 days of a verified request; nothing is kept longer than that except the de-identified counterparty records described above.

Children

BarterGrid is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from them.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.
